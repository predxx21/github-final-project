# Simple Interest Calculator

This repository contains a simple script to calculate simple interest. It is designed for educational purposes, as part of a Git and GitHub course assignment.

## About Simple Interest

Simple interest is a quick and straightforward method to calculate the interest charge on a loan or the return on an investment. It is always calculated based on the original principal amount. [citation:2]

### Formula

The formula for calculating simple interest is:

`Simple Interest (SI) = (P × R × T) / 100`

Where:
- **P** is the Principal amount (the initial sum of money).
- **R** is the Annual rate of interest (in percentage).
- **T** is the Time period (in years).

The total amount (A) to be paid back or received can be calculated as:
`A = P + SI`

### Example

Let's say you invest **$1000** (P) at an annual interest rate of **5%** (R) for **2 years** (T).

1.  **Calculate the Interest:**
    SI = (1000 × 5 × 2) / 100 = **$100**

2.  **Calculate the Total Amount:**
    A = 1000 + 100 = **$1100**

After 2 years, you would have a total of $1100, which includes your initial $1000 and $100 in interest earned.
